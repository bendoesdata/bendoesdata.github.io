About

My name is Benjamin, but most people just stick to Ben (which I'm cool with). I tell visual stories with data through a combination of data design, journalism and data science.

I started out in the journalism world circa 2016 before taking a deep dive into data at the Goldsmiths MSc Digital Journalism program. Now I make data-driven stories, content and visualizations with the infogr8 team.

Based in London, I spend most of my time thinking about how to communicate with data, how to humanise data and how to make data more accessible through smart visualisation.

I also write a weekly data newsletter with links to resources on data visualisation, data science and technology. You can signup for that here.

Feel like chatting? I tweet and email occasionally. You can also find my latest CV here.
