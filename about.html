About

My name is Benjamin, but most people just stick to Ben (which I'm cool with). I'm a data journalist telling visually engaging stories with data. I started out in the journalism world circa 2016 before taking a deep dive into data at the Goldsmiths MSc Digital Journalism program. Now I make data-driven stories, content and visualizations with the infogr8 team.

Based in London, I spend most of my time thinking about how to better use data, how to humanise data and how to make data more accessible through smart visualisation.

Feel like chatting? I tweet and email occasionally.
