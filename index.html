I work with teams to design and develop data visualizations.

↓

My work revolves around one central goal: making data visual. Often, this includes designing charts and graphs to communicate analytical results. Other times, it includes creating interfaces for exploring and discovering data. Whatever the application, my goal is to make data more tangible, understandable, and human.

I also create generative art with code and other visual experiments.You can find me tweeting, writing data-related blogs and sharing art made with code.
